Visit the waza national park

Waza National Park, nestled in Cameroon's Far North Region, spans 1,700 sq km of diverse landscapes. Home to elephants, giraffes, lions, and myriad bird species, it's renowned for wildlife encounters.

Visitors relish safari tours, birdwatching, and cultural immersions. With Sahelian savannahs and semi-arid terrains, the park offers a unique glimpse into the Sahel's natural beauty. For nature enthusiasts and wildlife aficionados, Waza National Park promises unforgettable experiences in a stunning African wilderness.

Mount Cameroon National park

Mount Cameroon National Park, situated in southwest Cameroon, encompasses the iconic Mount Cameroon, Africa's highest peak. Spanning diverse ecosystems from tropical rainforests to montane grasslands, the park boasts rich biodiversity, including unique flora and fauna.

Visitors can embark on exhilarating hikes to the summit, encountering volcanic landscapes and panoramic vistas along the way. The park offers opportunities for wildlife spotting, birdwatching, and cultural experiences with local communities. With its breathtaking scenery and adventurous trails, Mount Cameroon National Park beckons adventurers and nature enthusiasts alike to explore its majestic beauty.

KORUP National park

Korup National Park, located in southwestern Cameroon, is renowned as one of Africa's oldest and most biodiverse rainforests. Covering over 1,260 square kilometers, it harbors a treasure trove of plant and animal species, including rare primates, vibrant birdlife, and diverse flora.

Visitors can immerse themselves in the lush greenery and pristine wilderness through guided hikes, canopy walks, and wildlife safaris. With its towering trees, cascading waterfalls, and endemic species, Korup National Park offers a captivating glimpse into the heart of Africa's tropical rainforest, making it a haven for eco-tourism and nature enthusiasts.
